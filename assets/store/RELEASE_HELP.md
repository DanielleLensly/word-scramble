### How to use these for your release:

1. **Download the images**: You can save these images from the chat or locate them in the project artifacts directory.
2. **Set the App Icon in Flutter**:
   - To make the owl icon appear on your phone's home screen, you can use the `flutter_launcher_icons` package which is already in your project.
   - Simply save the App Icon as `assets/icon/icon.png` in your project folder.
   - Run this command: `flutter pub run flutter_launcher_icons`
3. **Upload to Play Console**:
   - When filling out your "Main store listing" in the Google Play Console, upload the **App Icon** to the "App icon" slot and the **Feature Graphic** to the "Feature graphic" slot.
