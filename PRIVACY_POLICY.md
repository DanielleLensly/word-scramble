# Privacy Policy for Kids Scramble Quest

**Last Updated: March 16, 2026**

Goldilocs Dev ("we," "us," or "our") operates the **Kids Scramble Quest** mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
**Kids Scramble Quest** is designed with children's privacy in mind. We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to external servers.

### Camera and Images
The app requires access to your device's camera and photo gallery to allow you to import spelling lists. 
*   **On-Device Processing**: Text recognition (OCR) is performed entirely on your device using Google ML Kit. 
*   **No Uploads**: Images captured or selected are used solely for text extraction and are **never** uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.

### Files and Storage
The app allows you to import `.pdf`, `.docx`, `.xlsx`, and `.txt` files. 
*   The app reads these files locally to extract words.
*   The app uses local storage (Shared Preferences) to save your "Past Uploads" history. This data remains on your device and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

## 2. Third-Party Services
We use Google ML Kit for text recognition. While this library is provided by Google, the processing for this specific app is configured to happen **locally on the device**. We do not use any third-party analytics (like Firebase Analytics) or advertising SDKs.

## 3. Children's Privacy
Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13 in a way that collects information. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us; however, our app is architected specifically to avoid such collection.

## 4. Security
We value your trust in providing us access to your camera and files, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
